FAQ

What is SpoilIt?

SpoilIt gives you instant movie and TV show recaps — plot beats, character fates, story flow, and moral themes — all in one place.

How many free recaps do I get?

You get 2 free recaps per day. Rate the app to unlock 1 bonus recap. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited recaps.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Movies and recaps are stored locally on your device. You can browse and read recaps without an internet connection.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → SpoilIt → Cancel
Android: Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → SpoilIt → Cancel

How do I delete my data?

Open the app → Settings (gear icon) → Clear Cache. To delete your account, email us at oxielabs@gmail.com.
