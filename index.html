SpoilIt Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

SpoilIt ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by OxieLabs. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

SpoilIt collects minimal data to provide and improve the service:

  • Usage Analytics: We track which recaps are viewed and shared (anonymously) to understand which content is popular. No personal identifiers are attached.
  • Search Queries: When you search for a movie or show that isn't in our database, we log the search query (not who searched) so we can add popular content.
  • Account Data: If you create an account, we store your email address and username for authentication purposes.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription purchases are handled by Apple (App Store) and Google (Play Store). We receive subscription status but not payment details.

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect contacts or phone data
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or trackers

Local Storage

The app stores movie and recap data locally on your device (using SQLite) for offline access. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted. Preferences like notification settings are stored locally using AsyncStorage.

Children's Privacy

SpoilIt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us. Local data can be cleared from the app's settings (Clear Cache).

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
